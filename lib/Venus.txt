2.1 Physical Properties and Orbital Dynamics
2.1.1 Basic Physical Properties
Mass: 4.8675 × 10²⁴ kg
Volume: 9.2843 × 10¹¹ km³
Equatorial radius: 6,051.8 km
Polar radius: 6,051.8 km
Surface gravity: 8.87 m/s²
Escape velocity: 10.36 km/s
Moment of inertia factor: 0.341
Surface pressure: 92,000 kPa

Semi-major axis: 108,208,000 km
Orbital period: 224.701 days
Average orbital speed: 35.02 km/s
Mean anomaly: 50.115°
Orbital inclination: 3.394°
Longitude of ascending node: 76.680°
Argument of perihelion: 54.884°
2.2 Atmospheric Science
2.2.1 Composition
Carbon dioxide (CO₂): 96.5%
Nitrogen (N₂): 3.5%
Sulfur dioxide (SO₂): 0.015%
Argon (Ar): 0.007%
Water vapor (H₂O): 0.002%
Carbon monoxide (CO): 0.0017%
2.2.2 Atmospheric Structure
Troposphere (0-65 km): Dense, hot, sulfuric acid clouds
Mesosphere (65-120 km): Temperature inversions
Thermosphere (120-220 km): Extreme temperature variations
Exosphere (>220 km): Gradual transition to space
2.3 Surface Features
2.3.1 Major Terrain Types
Volcanic Plains: Extensive lava flows
Highland Regions: Ishtar Terra, Aphrodite Terra
Corona Formations: Volcanic-tectonic features
Impact Basins: Mead Crater (largest impact crater)
2.3.2 Impact Features
Crater distribution affected by atmospheric shielding
Evidence of recent volcanic activity
